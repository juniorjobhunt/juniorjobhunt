About Junior Job Hunt

Built by students,
for the community

We started Junior Job Hunt because we saw two problems at once — neighbors who needed help, and young people who needed work. We fixed both.

Our Story

From a USD dorm room to a $15,000 award

Co-founders Brennan Martin and Ethan Addams were college students at the University of San Diego when they noticed something: their neighbors needed small tasks done, and their peers needed flexible ways to earn money.

What started as a simple idea grew into Junior Job Hunt — a platform connecting motivated local students with neighbors who need a helping hand. They took that idea all the way to the Fowler Business Concept Challenge, winning $15,000 to bring it to life.

🏆 $15,000 Fowler Business Concept Challenge Winners
Why Choose Us
Your satisfaction, guaranteed
We built Junior Job Hunt around one principle — trust. For customers, for taskers, and for the community.

Safe Environment for Taskers

We vet the adults doing the hiring — not the kids. Every customer is reviewed before being matched with a Tasker so our young workers always have a safe, trusted environment to earn in.

Community First

Founded by two USD college students, we understand what local youth need. Your hire directly supports a young person building real-world skills and earning meaningful income.

Affordable Rates

Because Taskers are local students — not large companies — rates are fair for both sides. Affordable for you, meaningful for them.

Vetted & Verified

Every Tasker goes through our screening process before being matched. You can hire with confidence knowing your Tasker has been reviewed and approved by our team.
