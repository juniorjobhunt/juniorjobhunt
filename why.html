Why Junior Job Hunt
Your satisfaction, guaranteed
We built Junior Job Hunt because we believe in community. Young people deserve flexible work opportunities — and neighbors deserve affordable, trustworthy help.

Safe Environment for Taskers

We vet the adults doing the hiring — not the kids. Every customer is reviewed before being matched with a Tasker so our young workers always have a safe, trusted environment to earn in.

Community First

Founded by two USD college students, we understand what local youth need. Your hire directly supports a young person building real-world skills.

Affordable Rates

Because Taskers are local students (not large companies), rates are fair for both sides — affordable for you, meaningful for them.

App Launching 2026

Our mobile app is on the way and launching in 2026 — making it even easier to hire a Tasker or pick up work in your neighborhood. For any questions reach us at juniorjobhunt@gmail.com
