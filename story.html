Our Story
Where it started
Junior Job Hunt was born out of a simple idea — what if the kid next door could be your go-to for everyday tasks? Co-founders Brennan Martin and Ethan Addams took that idea from a USD dorm room all the way to winning the Fowler Business Concept Challenge.
