Simple Process
Get help in 3 easy steps
Whether you need a one-time favor or ongoing help, Junior Job Hunt makes it simple to connect with a trusted local Tasker.
01

Post Your Task

Describe what you need — from lawn mowing to running errands. Include your location and timing preferences and submit your request in seconds.

02

Get Matched

We'll connect you with a nearby student Tasker who fits your needs. Every Tasker is screened and vetted before joining our platform.

03

Task Done!

Your Tasker shows up, gets the job done, and you pay a fair, affordable rate. You've helped a local student while getting your to-do list cleared.
