Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

JuniorJobHunt ("JuniorJobHunt," "we," "us," or "our") connects local students ("Taskers") with community members ("Customers") who need help with everyday tasks. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using juniorjobhunt.com (the "Site") or submitting a form, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly through our forms:

  • Taskers: name, email, phone number, age, school, neighborhood/city, skills, availability, and any short bio you choose to share.
  • Customers: name, email, phone number, neighborhood/city, task type, task description, preferred date, and urgency.
  • Waitlist: name, email, phone number, and city.

We also automatically receive limited technical information (such as IP address) that our hosting and form providers use to operate and protect the Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To introduce Taskers and Customers and facilitate a match;
  • To contact you about your submission and the service;
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site; and
  • To protect against spam, abuse, and fraud.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • To make a match, we share relevant contact and task details between the matched Tasker and Customer so they can arrange the work directly.
  • We use trusted service providers to run the Site, including Airtable (data storage), Cloudflare (hosting and form processing), and Resend (email delivery). They process information on our behalf.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, our users, or the public.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

4. Minors

Some Taskers are under 18. If you are under 18, you may use JuniorJobHunt only with the involvement and permission of a parent or legal guardian, and by submitting a form you confirm you have that permission. We collect only the information needed to make introductions. A parent or guardian may contact us at outreach@juniorjobhunt.com to review, correct, or delete their child's information.

5. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide the service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You can ask us to delete your information at any time (see "Your Choices and Rights").

6. Your Choices and Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you by emailing outreach@juniorjobhunt.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Third-Party Links

The Site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here with a new effective date.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Policy? Email us at outreach@juniorjobhunt.com.
